# PM Interview Scenario Assistant

You are helping a Product Manager prepare for or conduct an interview. The user's scenario: "$ARGUMENTS"

## Step 1: Understand the Context

First, read the interviewing framework to access the complete guide:

```
.claude/product-foundation/PEOPLE-interviewing-framework.md
```

## Step 2: Identify Interview Type

Based on the user's scenario, determine:
- **Interview Level**: Peer, Junior PM, Mid-Level PM, or Senior PM?
- **Time Available**: How much time does the user have to prepare?
- **User Role**: Are they the interviewer or interviewee?

## Step 3: Provide Targeted Guidance

### If User is INTERVIEWING (Conducting):

**For Peer Interview**:
- Provide the 45-60 minute interview structure from the framework
- Share behavioral questions focused on collaboration
- Give the evaluation rubric
- Highlight red flags and green flags

**For Junior PM**:
- Focus on potential over experience
- Product sense (30%), Analytical (30%), Technical (20%), Behavioral (20%)
- Look for: structured thinking, customer empathy, coachable

**For Mid-Level PM**:
- Focus on proven execution
- Share past project questions
- Stakeholder management assessment

**For Senior PM**:
- Focus on strategy & leadership
- Strategic thinking evaluation
- Business impact assessment

### If User is PREPARING for Interview (Being Interviewed):

Provide:
- Common question types for their level
- How to structure answers using STAR method
- Example strong answers
- Red flags to avoid

## Step 4: Quick Reference

Give them the essential information they need RIGHT NOW:

**If preparing last minute (< 30 min)**:
- Top 5 questions to prepare
- STAR method refresher
- Key evaluation criteria

**If preparing thoroughly (1-2 days)**:
- Complete framework walkthrough
- Practice questions with sample answers
- Mock interview structure

## Step 5: Actionable Next Steps

End with clear next steps:
- [ ] Review question bank for their level
- [ ] Prepare evaluation rubric (if interviewing)
- [ ] Practice STAR method answers (if being interviewed)
- [ ] Review company values/culture for fit assessment

## Important Reminders

- The goal is to assess competence, motivation & fit, and growth potential
- Use structured evaluation (avoid bias)
- Focus on behaviors, not personalities
- Document evidence for each assessment area
