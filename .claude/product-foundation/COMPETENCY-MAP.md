# PM Competency Map
**Scenario ‚Üí Competency ‚Üí Framework Mapping**

This document maps real-world PM scenarios to required competencies and relevant frameworks. Use this to find the right frameworks for any situation.

---

## How to Use This Map

**Step 1**: Find your scenario (or similar situation)
**Step 2**: Identify the competencies you need
**Step 3**: Use the recommended frameworks

**Example:**
- **Scenario**: "I'm about to conduct a peer interview"
- **Competencies**: Interviewing, Evaluation, Collaboration Assessment
- **Frameworks**: ‚Üí `PEOPLE-interviewing-framework.md`

---

## PM Competency Model

### Core PM Competencies (15 Total)

1. **Discovery & Research** - Understanding users and problems
2. **Prioritization** - Deciding what to build
3. **Validation** - Testing assumptions and fit
4. **Analysis** - Financial and market analysis
5. **Strategy** - Long-term planning and positioning
6. **Documentation** - Specifications and requirements
7. **Execution** - Delivery and goal-setting
8. **Launch** - Go-to-market and product launches
9. **Optimization** - Experimentation and testing
10. **Metrics** - Measurement and analytics
11. **Data Visualization & Analytics** - Chart selection, dashboard design, data storytelling
12. **User Psychology** - Engagement and behavior
13. **People Management** - Hiring, feedback, development
14. **Communication** - Stakeholder and influence
15. **Business Acumen** - Market sizing, ROI, strategy
