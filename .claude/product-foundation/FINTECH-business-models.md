## Core Fintech Business Models
## Các Mô Hình Kinh Doanh Fintech Cốt Lõi

### 1. Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)
### Mua Trước, Trả Sau

#### Business Model Mechanics
#### Cơ Chế Mô Hình Kinh Doanh

**Revenue Streams**:
- Merchant fees (2-8% per transaction)
- Late payment fees
- Premium subscription services

**Key Components**:
```
Consumer → BNPL Platform → Merchant
   ↓              ↓              ↓
Purchase → Credit Check → Instant Approval
   ↓              ↓              ↓
Repayment → Fee Collection → Product Delivery
```

**Risk Management**:
- Credit scoring algorithms
- Purchase pattern analysis
- Debt collection strategies

#### Test Question Coverage: Q36
**Which Fintech model provides BNPL?**
- **Answer**: Digital Lending (Category A)

#### Implementation Considerations
#### Cân Nhắc Triển Khai

- **Regulatory Compliance**: Consumer credit laws, data privacy
- **Partnership Strategy**: Merchant acquisition, payment processing
- **Technology Requirements**: Real-time credit assessment, fraud detection

---

### 2. Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Lending
#### Cho Vay Ngang Hàng

#### Business Model Mechanics
#### Cơ Chế Mô Hình Kinh Doanh

**Revenue Streams**:
- Origination fees (1-5% of loan amount)
- Service fees (monthly/annual)
- Interest rate spreads

**Platform Mechanics**:
```
Borrower → P2P Platform → Lender
   ↓           ↓              ↓
Application → Risk Assessment → Capital Offer
   ↓           ↓              ↓
Loan Funding → Repayment → Returns
```

**Trust Building Mechanisms**:
- Identity verification
- Credit history integration
- Social proof and ratings
- Escrow services

#### Test Question Coverage: Q37
**Group-based lending model?**
- **Answer**: Peer-to-Peer Lending (Category C)

#### Implementation Considerations
#### Cân Nhắc Triển Khai

- **Legal Framework**: Lending regulations, investor protection
- **Risk Management**: Default rates, diversification requirements
- **Technology**: Matching algorithms, payment processing

---

### 3. WealthTech & Robo-Advisory
#### Công Nghụ Tài Sản và Tư Vấn Robot

#### Business Model Mechanics
#### Cơ Chế Mô Hình Kinh Doanh

**Revenue Streams**:
- Management fees (0.25-2.5% of AUM)
- Performance fees
- Subscription models

**Service Components**:
```
Investor → Robo-Advisor → Financial Markets
   ↓           ↓                ↓
Risk Profile → Algorithm → Portfolio Allocation
   ↓           ↓                ↓
Investment → Monitoring → Rebalancing
```

**Investment Accessibility Strategies**:
- Low minimum investments ($1-100)
- Micro-investment options
- Fractional shares
- Educational content

#### Test Question Coverage: Q39
**Goal of small investment amounts?**
- **Answer**: Reduce entry barriers for new/financially limited users (Category C)

---

### 4. Payment Systems & Interchange Fees
#### Hệ Thống Thanh Toán và Phí Giao Dịch

#### Business Model Mechanics
#### Cơ Chế Mô Hình Kinh Doanh

**Interchange Fee Structure**:
```
Merchant → Acquiring Bank → Card Network → Issuing Bank
   ↓              ↓              ↓              ↓
Transaction → Processing Fee → Interchange Fee → Settlement
```

**Fee Components**:
- **Interchange Fee**: 1.3-3.5% (card issuer)
- **Assessment Fee**: 0.11-0.13% (card network)
- **Processor Fee**: 0.1-0.5% (payment processor)

#### Test Question Coverage: Q38
**Where do interchange fees appear?**
- **Answer**: Card transactions between merchants and issuing banks (Category B)

### Yêu Cầu Tuân Thủ

## Success Metrics
## Chỉ Số Thành Công

### Key Performance Indicators
### Chỉ Số Hiệu Suất Chính

| Metric | Target | Measurement Frequency |
|--------|--------|----------------------|
| **User Acquisition** | 10,000+ active users | Monthly |
| **Transaction Volume** | $1M+ monthly | Monthly |
| **Revenue Growth** | 20% MoM | Monthly |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost** | <$50 | Quarterly |
| **Lifetime Value** | >$500 | Quarterly |
| **Default Rate** | <3% (P2P) | Monthly |
| **Regulatory Compliance** | 100% | Ongoing |

### Financial Metrics
### Chỉ Số Tài Chính

- **Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)**: Total transaction volume
- **Net Revenue**: After fees and chargebacks
- **Contribution Margin**: Revenue minus direct costs
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Long-term profitability per user

## Risk Management
## Quản Lý Rủi Ro

### Key Risk Categories
### Các Loại Rủi Ro Chính

#### 1. Credit Risk
- **Assessment**: Credit scoring models, historical data analysis
- **Mitigation**: Diversification, reserve requirements, insurance

#### 2. Operational Risk
- **Assessment**: System reliability, process efficiency
- **Mitigation**: Redundancy, automation, monitoring

#### 3. Regulatory Risk
- **Assessment**: Regulatory changes, compliance gaps
- **Mitigation**: Legal counsel, compliance teams, adaptive systems

#### 4. Technology Risk
- **Assessment**: Cybersecurity threats, system failures
- **Mitigation**: Security protocols, disaster recovery, regular audits
