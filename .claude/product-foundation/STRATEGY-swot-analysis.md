**What is SWOT?**
- **S**trengths: Internal advantages (what you're good at)
- **W**eaknesses: Internal disadvantages (what you're not good at)
- **O**pportunities: External favorable conditions (what you could exploit)
- **T**hreats: External unfavorable conditions (what could harm you)

### Step 7: Create Strategies (45 minutes)

**Use TOWS Matrix (Advanced SWOT)**

TOWS = Reverse SWOT, creates actionable strategies:

```
              STRENGTHS           WEAKNESSES

OPPORTUNITIES  SO Strategies      WO Strategies
              (Aggressive)        (Turnaround)
              Use strengths to    Overcome weaknesses
              seize opportunities to seize opportunities
```

## Common Mistakes / Sai Láº§m ThÆ°á»ng Gáº·p

### Mistake 1: Confusing Internal vs External
**Problem**: Listing "competitors" as weakness (it's a threat)

**Why It Matters**:
- Strengths/Weaknesses = Internal (you control)
- Opportunities/Threats = External (market forces)

**Solution**:
- Internal: Product, team, resources, processes
- External: Market, competition, regulation, trends

### Mistake 2: Too Generic
**Problem**: "Strong team" or "Market is growing"

**Why It Fails**: Not actionable, could apply to anyone

**Solution**: Be specific
- âŒ "Strong team"
- âœ… "3 ex-Googlers with 10+ years in ML, built similar system at scale"

### Mistake 3: Only Focusing on Positives
**Problem**: 20 strengths, 2 weaknesses (not honest)

**Why It Fails**: Ignoring weaknesses doesn't make them disappear

**Solution**: Be brutally honest. Weaknesses are opportunities for improvement.

### Mistake 4: Analysis Paralysis
**Problem**: Perfect SWOT but no action

**Why It Fails**: SWOT is tool, not goal. Strategy is goal.

**Solution**: Always go from SWOT â†’ Strategies â†’ Action Plan

### Mistake 5: Doing It Once and Forgetting
**Problem**: SWOT from 2 years ago collecting dust

**Why It Fails**: Markets change, competitors evolve

**Solution**: Review quarterly. Update when major shifts happen.

### Mistake 6: Wrong Scope
**Problem**: Company SWOT when you need product SWOT

**Why It Fails**: Too broad or too narrow doesn't help decisions

**Solution**: Define scope upfront. Match SWOT to decision at hand.

### Mistake 7: No Prioritization
**Problem**: 50 items in SWOT, all equal weight

**Why It Fails**: Can't focus on everything

**Solution**: Prioritize top 3-5 in each quadrant. Focus on high-impact items.

### SWOT Process (2-4 hours)
1. **Prepare** (30 min): Context, team, scope
2. **Strengths** (20 min): What we're good at
3. **Weaknesses** (20 min): What we lack
4. **Opportunities** (20 min): What we could exploit
5. **Threats** (20 min): What could harm us
6. **Prioritize** (30 min): High/medium/low impact
7. **Strategies** (45 min): TOWS matrix
8. **Action Plan** (30 min): Who, when, what, metrics

### TOWS Strategy Matrix
- **SO**: Use strengths to seize opportunities (Aggressive)
- **WO**: Overcome weaknesses to seize opportunities (Improvement)
- **ST**: Use strengths to counter threats (Diversification)
- **WT**: Minimize weaknesses and avoid threats (Defensive)
